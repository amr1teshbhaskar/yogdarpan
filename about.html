How we relate

Yoga is like a darpan (mirror) through which we can see our own Image (self). Yoga darpan shows the way to understand importance of body fitness, mind stillness and one's spirit. Yogdarpan lights on self realization,self illumination, understanding truth and living harmonious life, without harmony self control and self-discipline creates more conflict rather than peace
